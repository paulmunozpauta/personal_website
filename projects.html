Research Projects

  • Data fusion of near-real-time satellite products for improving runoff forecasting
    Ecuador & Belgium — 2023–2025
    Integration of satellite precipitation and AI models for operational flood forecasting.
  • Development of runoff and flash flood forecasting models for Andean basins
    Ecuador — 2019–2023
    Machine learning models trained with local hydrometeorological and radar data.
  • Hydrological forecasting models using weather radar data
    Ecuador — 2021–2023
    Exploiting high-resolution radar inputs for peak flow predictions in mountainous basins.
  • Evapotranspiration estimation methods in a páramo ecosystem
    Ecuador — 2018–2019
    Comparison of remote sensing and in-situ approaches in a high-altitude Andean microcatchment.
  • High-resolution radar analysis of precipitation extremes and ENSO dynamics
    Ecuador & Northern Peru — 2022–2024
    Assessment of extreme rainfall behavior and teleconnections using radar data.
  • Trans-American research network for water resilience
    Americas (multi-country) — 2023–ongoing
    Collaborative initiative on headwater system resilience and downstream water security.
